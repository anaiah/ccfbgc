  • Christ-centered gatherings

Get Connected

Our Heart as CCF BGC

We are a Christ-centered community in the heart of BGC, calling people to know Jesus, grow in His Word, and go make disciples in every sphere of life.

Our Mission

To honor God and make Christ-committed followers who will make Christ-committed followers in Bonifacio Global City and beyond — people who love God fully, live out His Word, and intentionally share the gospel wherever He has placed them.

Our Vision

To see a movement of disciples and small groups across BGC — families, professionals, students, and workplaces — transformed by the gospel, serving together so that every person in our city has a clear opportunity to know and follow Jesus.
